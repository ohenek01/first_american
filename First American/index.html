The First American Specialist Medical Center is a dedicated team of board certified and fellowship trained surgeons and physicians who provide an extensive range of treatments for disorders of the nervous system and spine. In addition, we offer a broad spectrum of multi-disciplinary treatment options for severe pain disorders.

The First American Specialist Medical Center is dedicated to its founding principles of patient-focused care and to providing a sensitive and compassionate touch throughout every stage of a patient's treatment.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to provide the highest quality comprehensive surgical and pain management treatment to improve the health and well-being of individuals, families and our communities. We are committed to meeting the quality and service expectations of our patients.

    Our Values
  • Putting the patient first
  • Quality care for patients
  • Respectful, compassionate care
  • Continuous Quality Improvement
  • Integrity
    Our Vision

    Through the services we provide, First American Specialist Medical Center will consistently be recognized by our patients and referring physicians as one of the best neurosurgical and pain medical centers, providing one stop shop for most neurological conditions.
